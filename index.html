Why this guide?

We've all been there before. You arrive in a new, huge city, confused and on a tight schedule. You consult lonely planet and wikitravel and come up short - They don't know what I like! This is a guide personally tailored for Zoe Ofer, with all her quirks and inclinations taken into account.

Thanks

Cafes & Bakeries

All of the cups of coffee and croissants you could possibly need in two weeks.

My Little Cup

Created by two charming French lovers of coffee, this coffee shop in the pretty decoration proposes a breakfast formula (from 7:30 to 11 am) at 7,50 € including a hot drink, fruit juice and Viennoiserie (a piece of baguette with jam or other cake). Hot chocolate and cappucino are also very successful, cookies and homemade cakes rather good. Open Monday through Friday.
